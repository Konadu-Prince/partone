Overview

Discover the breathtaking beauty of Canada, from the majestic Rocky Mountains to the vibrant cities of Toronto and Vancouver. This adventure takes you through some of the most stunning landscapes in North America, offering a perfect blend of outdoor activities and urban exploration.

Experience world-class skiing, hiking through pristine wilderness, and exploring charming mountain towns. Visit iconic landmarks like Niagara Falls, Banff National Park, and the CN Tower while immersing yourself in Canadian culture and hospitality.

Trip Highlights

Rocky Mountain Adventure

Hike through Banff and Jasper National Parks, witness stunning glaciers and crystal-clear lakes.

Urban Exploration

Explore Toronto's CN Tower, Vancouver's Stanley Park, and Montreal's historic Old Town.

Niagara Falls

Experience the power and beauty of one of the world's most famous waterfalls.

Winter Sports

Enjoy world-class skiing and snowboarding at Whistler and other premier resorts.

Itinerary

1

Day 1-2: Toronto Arrival

Arrive in Toronto, explore the CN Tower, visit the Royal Ontario Museum, and enjoy the vibrant food scene in Kensington Market.

2

Day 3-4: Niagara Falls

Take a day trip to Niagara Falls, experience the Hornblower cruise, and explore the charming town of Niagara-on-the-Lake.

3

Day 5-7: Banff National Park

Fly to Calgary and drive to Banff. Hike to Lake Louise, explore the town of Banff, and take the gondola to Sulphur Mountain.

4

Day 8-10: Vancouver

Fly to Vancouver, explore Stanley Park, visit Granville Island, and enjoy the city's diverse culinary scene.
